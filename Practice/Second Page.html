A little about Bananas

What are bananas?

Bananas are a tropical fruit that is extremely popular and loved worldwide. They are sweet,soft and easy to eat on the go.

Benefits of Eating Bananas

  • Rich in potassium
  • Good for digestion
  • Boosts Energy
  • Help manage blood pressure
